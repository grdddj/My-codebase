Blockchain roulette

Idea: To create a transparent gambling applications with clearly defined chance of winning.

Functioning: After filling the data transaction is generated and will send the bet amount on the contract address. Potential win is then authomatically sent back to the user.

Advantages: Source code of randomness is can be viewed publicly, and therefore players can collectively scrutinize it.

Disadvantages: Each bet forms a transaction and that means transaction fees to be paid. Easy access to hazard can lead to addiction*.
