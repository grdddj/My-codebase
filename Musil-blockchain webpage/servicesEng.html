Services

I am offering courses on various topics regarding blockchain and cryptocurrencies. Possible delivery of these courses could be Skype interview or other videoconference service - I prefer direct interaction with the possibility of asking questions any time. However, it could be also possible to receive prerecorded videos from the course.

If you are interested in any of the courses, please let me know via e-mail musil-blockchain@seznam.cz and we can discuss the details.

    Philosophy of the blockchain

    Before learning about the technical details of the blockchain, it is very important to understand why do we even need it, why it can be beneficial to use blockchain and in which applications it brings the highest value.

  • The importance of cryptocurrencies and other use cases of blockchain

    Explore the possible impacts and benefits of cryptocurrencies for the ordinary people, and find out about other promising applications of blockchain.

    You will learn about: centralised and decentralised systems, technology of money, history of Bitcoin, significance of open-source projects or future of blockchain.


    • Technical background and blockchain programming

    These courses are focused on deep understanding the blockchain technology and basic development of blockchain applications.

  • How does blockchain and cryptocurrencies work

    On the specific example of Bitcoin blockchain, you will get a detailed explanation of all important concepts and how it all works together to create one big ecosystem.

    You will learn about: decentralised architecture, cryptography and keys, digital signatures, transactions, consensus mechanisms or mining.

  • The world of decentralized apps

    On the example of Ethereum blockchain, you will find out how blockchain platforms work and how are they used in creating decentralised applications.

    You will learn about: decentralised apps, Ethereum platform, Cryptokitties, Metamask extension or smart contracts.

  • Creating a token on Ethereum platform - your own cryptocurrency

    In practical example, you will learn how to easily create a token on Ethereum platform.

    You will learn about: Remix development environment, Solidity programming language, creating tokens or deploying smart contracts.


    • Cryptocurrencies, trading and investment

    This series of courses will introduce you the economic aspects of cryptocurrencies and how to safely become active in trading and investing.

  • How to buy your first cryptocurrencies and trade with them

    You will find out what are the possibilities of cryptocurrencies trading today, and set up an account on the most secure exchanges.

    You will learn about: Bitcoin and altcoins, various exchanges, CoinMarketCap, security or potential risks.

  • How to secure your cryptocurrencies and manipulate with them safely

    In these early days of cryptocurrencies it is not always easy not to make a mistake and lose your funds - you will find out, how to prevent this.

    You will learn about: private keys, addresses, wallets and their multiple types, hackers or decentralised exchanges.

  • How not to get lost in ICO jungle

    ICO or Initial Coin Offering is one of the most successful methods of fundraising ever created. However, it is also one of the most dangerous ones for investors, and it is very easy to lose money. Let's find out how to prevent bad investment decisions.

    You will learn about: utility and security tokens, platforms for ICOs, whitepapers, its important details or common red flags.
